About

Bill Gates is a renowned philanthropist and co-founder of Microsoft. He is known for his contributions to the world of technology and his efforts to improve global healthcare and education.

Life of Bill Gates

Bill Gates was born on October 28, 1955 in Seattle, Washington. Bill Gates was originally named William Henry Gates. Bill has two sisters named Libby and Kristianne. When he was 13 years old he started to have an interest in computers. When he was only 13, Bill Gates created a tic-tac-toe game that allowed people to play against the computer. During his time in high school Bill Gates met Paul Allen, who soon became one of his closest friends. They worked together on computer programs and software. When Bill Gates took the SATs he scored a 1590 out of 1600, one of the best scores ever seen in history. He went to college at Harvard University, a prestigious ivy-league school.

Originally, in college Gates was going to study law, but he spent so much of his time in the computer lab that his major soon changed. Throughout college Gates and Allen kept in touch with each other. Both Bill Gates and Paul Allen met in 1974 at Honeywell. While there they saw a min-computer, called the Altair 8800. Both were so intrigued by it that they called MITS and asked Ed Roberts if he wanted someone to invent software to run the computer. They worked on it for two months and when they showed it to Ed Roberts, it worked like a charm.

In 1975, Bill Gates and Paul Allen became partners and named themselves Micro-Soft, a mixture of software and micro-computer. In 1978, when Bill Gates was only 23, he made himself the head of Microsoft. During the years of 1978-1981, Microsoft grew from 25 employees to 128 employees, and the revenue of Microsoft went from 4 million dollars to 16 million dollars. In 1985, Microsoft released Windows. By the age of 31, Bill Gates was a millionaire, and one year later he was a billionaire. Bill Gates is currently 58 years old and has three kids named Rory John Gates, Jennifer Katharine Gates, and Phoebe Adele Gates. He also has a wife named Melinda Gates.
